# Join the HyperAgility Movement

HyperAgility is more than just a manifesto; it's a community of developers, engineers, and leaders redefining how we build software in the age of AI.

## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions to the manifesto itself. If you have ideas, corrections, or want to translate the manifesto into other languages, please get involved.

- **GitHub:** [Submit a Pull Request or Issue](https://github.com/hyperagility)
- **Discussion:** Join the conversation on our GitHub Discussions board.

## Get in Touch

Have questions, feedback, or want to share your own experiences with AI-driven development? We'd love to hear from you.
